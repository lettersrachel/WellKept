01

Getting in

  1. Open wellkept-orcin.vercel.app on any device.
  2. Type your email — lettersrachel@gmail.com — and choose Send magic link.
  3. Open your inbox, click the link, and you're in. The link works once and expires in 24 hours.

Today the link only reaches your own inbox. Once your domain's DNS records go live, it will email anyone you invite.

02

Your home screen — the Fleet board

A list of every household you oversee — right now Fernbrook and Chen-Williams. Each row shows its health at a glance: status tag, how complete the playbook is, visits logged, stranger-test result. Click a household name to open it in full.

03

What you can do inside a household

Set the status tag

Flip a household to LIFE-EVENT and every gift prompt across the system goes quiet. Flip it back, they return. Holds — never deletes.

Review client suggestions

When a client proposes an update it lands in a queue. Approve & merge, or decline. Nothing changes silently.

Reveal a vault item

Secured details — alarm and gate codes — sit behind a "Reveal (logged)" button. Every peek is recorded and auto-hides after 60 seconds.

Add people

The People & access card: type an email, pick a role, done. This is how you add Kelly or a new house manager yourself.

See what's coming

The anticipation panel lists auto-scheduled prompts — like Mia's birthday radar — and the change log reads in plain sentences.

Export the numbers

"Export exhibit CSV" downloads a fleet spreadsheet in the shape your investor and SBA packets already use.

04

The one thing to try

Put two screens side by side

Open Lisa's screen and your screen on the same household. Mia's allergy details and "tooth fairy is active for Owen" are on your screen and completely absent from Lisa's — not hidden by styling, never sent to her at all.
